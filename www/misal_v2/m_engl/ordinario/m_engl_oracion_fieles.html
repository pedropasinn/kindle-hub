UNIVERSAL PRAYER

1.   GENERAL FORMULA I

CELEBRANT
God, our Father, has brought us here together as his family. Let us now humbly ask him for all our needs.
1. For the holy Church of God: that the Lord may guide and protect it. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. For all the people of the world: that the Lord may unite them in peace and harmony. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. For all our brothers and sisters in need: that the Lord may assist them. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. Let us commend ourselves, and all God's people, living and dead, to the intercession of our Lady, the glorious and ever Virgin Mother of God.
R/. Hail Mary…

CELEBRANT
God of love, our refuge and our strength, hear the prayers of your Church, and grant us today what we ask of you in faith. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
2.   GENERAL FORMULA II

CELEBRANT
My brothers and sisters, God our Father wants all mankind to be saved and calls us to the knowledge of the truth. Let us pray to him with all our hearts.
1a. Protect and guide your Church, especially our Holy Father, Pope …, our (Arch)Bishop …, and all your faithful people. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2a. Guide those who govern our country that they may always honour the laws of God and so rule with wisdom and justice. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2b. Grant that the laws of each country may respect the laws of God and all citizens may respect those just laws. Lord hear us.
R/. .
3a. Teach those who suffer to offer their suffering joyfully to you. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3b. Grant eternal rest to those who have died, or will die soon. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4a. Grant us here present to sanctify ourselves in daily work and on the fulfilment of the ordinary duties of a Christian. Teach us to light up the paths of the earth with faith and love. Lord hear us..
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4b. Let us commend ourselves, and all God's people, living and dead, to the intercession of our Lady, the glorious and ever Virgin Mother of God.
R/. Hail Mary…

CELEBRANT
God of love, our refuge and our strength, hear the prayers of your Church, and grant us today what we ask of you in faith. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
3.   ADVENT SEASON

CELEBRANT
As we prepare for the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, let us earnestly ask his mercy. He came into the world to preach the good news to the poor and to heal the repentant sinners. Let us ask him to come again to our world today, bringing salvation to all who stand in need.
1. That the Lord Jesus may enrich with spiritual gifts our Pope N., the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese N, and all bishops. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. That the Lord Jesus may guide those in authority to follow his will and to seek the good of all mankind. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. That the Lord Jesus may heal the sick, rid the world of hunger, and protect us from all disasters. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. That the Lord Jesus may touch our hearts with deep sorrow for our sins with a firm purpose of amendment and teach us to find strength in frequent confession and communion. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Almighty, ever-living God, your will for mankind is that none should be lost and all should be saved. Hear the prayers of your people, guide the course of the world in your peace and let your Church serve you in tranquillity and joy. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
4.   CHRISTMAS SEASON

CELEBRANT
Today (tonight, at this season), the kindness and love of God our Saviour has appeared among us. Let us offer our prayers to God, not trusting in our own good deeds, but in his love for mankind.
1. For the Church of God: that the Lord Jesus may enrich with spiritual gifts our Pope …, the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese …, and all your faithful people. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. For the peace and well-being of the whole world: that God's gifts to us in this life lead us to salvation in the life to come. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. For those who suffer from hunger, sickness, or loneliness: that the mystery of Christ's birth bring them health and peace. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. For all of us and our families, who welcome Christ in our lives: that we may learn to receive him in the poor and suffering people of this world. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Lord God, Mary gave birth to your Son Jesus Christ, our Lord, in purity and love. May she bring our prayers before you, for we make them in the name of Jesus the Lord.
R/. Amen.
5.   LENT SEASON

CELEBRANT
We should pray at all times, but especially during this season of Lent: we should faithfully keep watch with Christ and pray to our Father.
1. That our Lord Jesus Christ may enrich with spiritual gifts our Pope …, the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese …, all your faithful people and those who will be baptised at Easter. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. That people everywhere may work for peace to make these days the acceptable time. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. That all who have sinned or grown lukewarm may turn to God again during this time of reconciliation. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. That we ourselves may learn to repent and turn from sin with all our hearts seeking strength in frequent confession and communion. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Lord, may your people turn again and serve you with all their hearts. With confidence we have asked your help: may we now know your mercy and love in our lives. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
7.   HOLY WEEK

CELEBRANT
On these days when Christ prayed and entreated his Father in the anguish of his passion, let us pray to the Lord with humility and sorrow for our sins. May our Father hear and answer our prayers out of love for his Son, Jesus Christ.
1. For God's Church, the Bride of Christ, that she may be purified by his blood. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. For the world in which we live, that God may give us health and peace through the blood of Christ. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. For the sick and suffering, that God may give them courage and strength to share the suffering of Christ. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. For believers and unbelievers everywhere, for all our brothers and sisters around the world, that the suffering and death of our Lord Jesus Christ may lead us to the glory of rising again. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Father, be near to your people and hear our prayers. We have many needs that we cannot express, but you know them, and we ask you to help us through the sufferings and death of your Son Jesus Christ, who is Lord for ever and ever.
R/. Amen.
8.   EASTER SEASON

CELEBRANT
With joy at Christ's rising from the dead, let us turn to God our Father in prayer. He heard and answered the prayers of the Son he loved so much: let us trust that he will hear our petitions.
1. That the Lord Jesus may enrich with spiritual gifts our Pope …, the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese …, and all your faithful people. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. That the whole world may rejoice in the blessings of true peace, the peace Christ himself gives us. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. That our suffering brothers and sisters may have their sorrow turned into joy. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. That we all may have the faith and strength to bear witness to Christ's resurrection. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Father, you know the many different needs your people have in this life. Hear us and answer the prayers of all who believe in you. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
9.   ORDINARY TIME, I

CELEBRANT
Gathered together in Christ as brothers and sisters, let us call to mind God's many blessings and ask him to hear the prayers which he himself inspires us to ask.
1. For N., our Pope, for N., the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese, for all the Church's ministers and for all the people they have been called to lead and serve. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. For those who serve us in public office and for all those entrusted with the common good. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. For all travellers, by land, air, or sea; for prisoners, and for all those unjustly deprived of freedom. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. For all of us gathered in this holy place in faith, reverence, and love of God. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Father, hear the prayers of your Church. In your love, make up for what is lacking in our faith. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
10.   ORDINARY TIME, II

CELEBRANT
Dear brothers and sisters: let us pray to God, the almighty Father, who wishes all men to be saved and to come to the knowledge of the truth.
1. For the Holy Catholic Church, that, faithful to her mission, she may be the leaven of society and bring God's love to all mankind. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. For our beloved Pope N., for the (Arch)Bishop of this diocese, that they may govern and care for those entrusted to them. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. For those in need, that they receive spiritual and material help and be strengthened by God's grace. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4. For those here present, that we may reflect Christ's redeeming love and always remain in the truth. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
O God, our refuge and our strength, hear the prayers of your Church, and grant that what we ask with faith we may be worthy to receive. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
R/. Amen.
11.   MASSES FOR THE DEAD

CELEBRANT
Let us pray with faith and confidence to God our Father who lives for ever and who can do all things. He raised his Son, Jesus Christ, from death. May he give peace and salvation to the living and the dead.
1. That God may bring all Christians together in unity and faith. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
2. That God may take the evil of war away from our world. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
3. That God may show himself a Father to those who lack food, work, and shelter. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4a. That N., who once began eternal life in Baptism, may join the company of the saints for ever. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.
4f. That all gathered here to show their faith and love may be reunited in the glory of God's Kingdom. Lord hear us.
R/. Lord, graciously hear us.

CELEBRANT
Lord Jesus Christ, hear our prayers for our brother (sister) N., and for all who have gone before us in faith to eternal life. Free them from all their sins and let them share in the fullness of salvation in the kingdom where you are Lord for ever and ever.
R/. Amen.
